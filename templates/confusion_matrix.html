Metric Formulas
Accuracy
(TP + TN) / Total
Overall correct rate
Precision
TP / (TP + FP)
Of predicted positives, how many are real?
Recall
TP / (TP + FN)
Of actual positives, how many were found?
F1-Score
2 × (P × R) / (P + R)
Harmonic mean of Precision & Recall
